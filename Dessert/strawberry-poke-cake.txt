Ingredients:
    - 1 box WHITE CAKE MIX
    - WATER, VEGETABLE OIL, EGGS, as directed on box
    
    - 1 Cup WATER
    - 1 box (4-serving size) strawberry JELL-O

    - Excessive amounts of WHIPPED CREAM
    - Cut STRAWBERRIES (optional)

Directions:
    1. Bake CAKE as directed on box
    2. Cool for 20 minutes
    3. Boil WATER and add JELLO
    4. Poke holes in CAKE with handle of wooden spoon
    5. Pour JELLO over CAKE
    6. Cool for 1 hour
    7. Spread WHIPPED CREAM over cake and add STRAWBERRIES
    8. Consume
