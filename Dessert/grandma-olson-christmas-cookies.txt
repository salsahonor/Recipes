Ingredients:
    - 2 Tbps BUTTER
    - 1 Cup CHOCOLATE CHIPS
    - 1 EGG
    - 1 Cup POWDERED SUGAR
    - 1 Cup chopped WALNUTS
    - 7-10.5 oz COLORED MARSHMALLOWS
    - 1 Cup crushed PEPPERMINT CANDY (optional)
    - crushed GRAHAM CRACKERS

Directions:
    1. Melt together BUTTER and CHOCOLATE CHIPS
    2. Cream together EGG and POWDERED SUGAR
    3. Combine with WALNUTS, CHOCOLATE, MARSHMALLOWS/CANDY
    4. Form into roll and then roll in GRAHAM CRACKERS
    5. Wrap in foil and freeze. Slice and serve frozen.
