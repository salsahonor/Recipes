Ingredients:
    - 3 Cans CREAM OF POTATO SOUP
    - 2 Cans EVAPORATED MILK
    - 1 Bag (2-3 Trees) BROCCOLI
    - 2 Cups grated (extra) SHARP CHEESE

Directions:
    1. Cook BROCCOLI
    2. Empty SOUP into large pot
    3. Slowly add MILK, stir out lumps
    4. Add cooked BROCCOLI (and maybe also some of the juice)
    5. Add CHEESE
    6. Stir until CHEESE is melted
    7. Consume
