Ingredients:
    - 2 Tbps OLIVE OIL (divided)
    - 1.5-2 lbs CHICKEN BREAST (cut 1-inch chunks)
    - 1/3 Cup CORNSTARCH
    - 1/2 tsp SALT, PEPPER
    
    - 1 Cup ORANGE MARMALADE
    - 1/4 Cup low-sodium SOY SAUCE
    - 1 Tbps RICE WINE VINEGAR
    - 1 tsp SESAME OIL
    - 1/2-1 tsp ground GINGER
    - 1/2 tsp GARLIC POWDER

Directions:
    1. Line a 3-quart+ slow cooker for easy clean up
    2. Drizzle 1 Tbps OLIVE OIL in slow cooker
    3. In a gallon-sized ziploc, combine CHICKEN, CORNSTARCH,
       SALT, PEPPER. Seal bag and toss.
    4. Add ziploc contents to slow cooker.
    5. In medium bowl, combine ORANGE MARMALADE, SOY SAUCE,
       RICE WINE VINEGAR, SESAME OIL, GINGER, GARLIC, OLIVE 
       OIL. Stir to combine.
    6. Pour mixture over chicken, toss. 
    7. Cook covered on high for 1.5- 2 hours or on low for 3-
       4 hours.
    8. Consume.
