Ingredients:
    - 1 Tbps BUTTER
    - 1 Tbps FLOUR
    - 3-4 LEEKS
    - 2 RUSSET POTATOES
    - 1/2 WHITE ONION
    - 1/2 Cup MILK
    - 4 Cups CHICKEN BROTH
    - SALT, PEPPER, CAYANNE to taste

Directions:
    1. Rinse and chop LEEKS, POTATOES, ONION
    2. Melt BUTTER in a large pot, add FLOUR and stir
    3. Add everything to pot
    4. Bring to a boil, simmer for 25 minutes. Blend.
